Our Story

A Journey of Faith

ACK St James Koromosho has been a pillar of faith in the Nakuru community for over 25 years. What began as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant congregation of over 500 members.

From humble beginnings in a rented hall, God has blessed us with a beautiful sanctuary and facilities that serve our community. We've witnessed countless lives transformed, families restored, and hearts renewed through the power of the Gospel.

Today, we continue to grow in faith, reaching out to our neighbors with the love of Christ, and preparing the next generation to carry the torch of faith into the future.
